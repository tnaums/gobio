CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine-type endopeptidase that cleaves Gly-Gly bonds in the
CC       polyglycine linker of host plant class IV chitinases to disrupt their
CC       chitin-binding, and thereby plays a role in lowering the defense
CC       responses of the host to the fungus (PubMed:24627966, PubMed:25966977,
CC       PubMed:35240278, PubMed:36762862). Degrades Z.mays Endochitinase A
CC       (CHIA) (PubMed:24627966, PubMed:25966977, PubMed:35240278,
CC       PubMed:36762862). Degrades Z.mays Endochitinase B (CHIB)
CC       (PubMed:24627966, PubMed:25966977). Has no activity on Z.mays CHIA
CC       following CHIA cleavage by fungalysin (PubMed:24627966).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24627966, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25966977,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:35240278, ECO:0000269|PubMed:36762862}.
